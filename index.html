#about-me

I came to development from a related profession: for 2 years I worked as a UX editor at Skyeng. I’ve always been interested in how websites and apps are structured from the inside out. Gradually I was fascinated by the desire not just to write texts for interfaces, think about the user’s path and influence the product, but also to create it myself.

Past experience has helped me learn to be creative, find common ground with different people and work for results. I’m looking forward to using these qualities in my new role.

Now I am studying web development at Yandex Practicum school, reading «Grokking algorithms», watching lectures by CS50, taking a React course on Udemy and working on my pet-projects.

In my spare time I am involved in music and learning foreign languages. I like to combine these two hobbies.
